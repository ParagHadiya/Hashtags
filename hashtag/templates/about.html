🙋‍♂️ About HashtagGen

HashtagGen is your smart assistant for discovering powerful, relevant hashtags with AI support.

HashtagGen is designed for creators, influencers, and marketers looking to enhance their content reach on platforms like Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Using AI and ML, we generate highly engaging and trend-specific hashtags based on your keywords.

We aim to empower you with tools like:

  • ✔️ AI-generated hashtags
  • ✔️ Trend analysis by platform
  • ✔️ Custom keyword-based hashtag groups
  • ✔️ Easy copy-to-clipboard UI
  • ✔️ A smooth, responsive, and animated experience

📈 Our Mission

We strive to democratize hashtag discovery by leveraging data and intelligence. Whether you're launching a product, promoting a cause, or growing your personal brand, HashtagGen simplifies your social strategy with smart hashtag generation.
